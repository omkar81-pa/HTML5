Shree Pawan Putra Hanuman

Hanuman also know as Maruti, Bajrangbali, and Anjaneya,is a deity in Hinduism, revered as a divine vanar, and a devoted companion of the deity Rama.

Birth

According to Hindu legends, Hanuman was born to mother Anjana and father Kwsari. Hanuman also called the son of the deity Vayu (wind god) because of lagends associated with Vayu's role in Hanuman birth and is said to be the incarnation of Shiv.

Childhood

According to Valmiki's Ramayana, one morning in this Childhood, Hanuman was hangey and saw the sun.Mistaking it for a ripe fruit, he leapt up to eat it.

Temples and shrines

Majore temples and shrines Hanuman:
The oldest knows independent Hanuman temple and statue is at Khajuraho, dated to about 922 CE from the Khajuraho Hanuman inscription.
Hanumangarhi, Ayodhya, is a 10th-century temple dedicated to Hanuman.
The Panchmukhi Hanuman Mandir is a 1500-year-old tempal in pakistan.
Jakhu templem in shimla, Himachal Pradesh contains a monumental 108-feet statue of Hanuman and is the height point in shimla.
The tallest Hanuma statue is the Veera Abhaya Anjaneya Swami, standing 135 feet tall at paritala, Andhra Pradesh.
